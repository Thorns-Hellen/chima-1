Malaria is a serious and sometimes fatal disease caused by a parasite that commonly infects a certain type of mosquito which feeds on humans!

Symptoms

Fever


This fever may reach as high as 104 F (40 C) and last for a couple of days.


Chills


These chills are another symptom that appears in the initial phase of the disease.


Headaches


Severe headaches sometimes occur along with the joint pain and fever.
